About Royal Bite

Kizhekethala's Local Feast Landmark

Rooted deeply in the food-loving heart of Karuvarakundu, Royal Bite was founded with a single mission: to serve premium, non-compromised Kuzhimandhi and Malabar Dum Biriyani.

Our secret is pure firewood pit-cooking, making sure every grain of rice absorbs the deep, rich smokiness and flavor that gas ovens simply cannot match. Every morning, our chefs painstakingly blend organic spices imported directly, combined with the freshest meat from local Karuvarakundu farms.

  • Pristine Hygiene & Open Kitchen Guidelines
  • Warm traditional Malabar Hospitality & Seating
  • Ample car and bike parking space in Kizhekethala
